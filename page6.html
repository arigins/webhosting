Mobile Wi-Fi Devices

Open your Wi-Fi settings on your computer or tablet and connect to the Mobile Wi-Fi Device Network.

Open your web browser and type 192.168.8.1 into your address bar.

Use these login details:
Username - admin
Password - admin

Navigate to the settings section and click on the APN tab**.

Enter afrihost into the APN field and click save*.

* IMPORTANT: Ensure all letters are lowercase and there are NO spaces before or after 'afrihost'.

** This function is not available on a mobi site, make sure to use your computer or tablet to log in.

Please Note that although in most cases the APN connection might propergate immediately, some clients have experienced cases where the connection might take longer to establish.

If your connections takes some time to establish please be sure to check whether your in an area with a bad network reception, or if there are no other decvices that could disturb your APN connection.   

If the issue still pursists, please check to see if your MTN Simcard has not sustained any kind of damage what so ever.

If there is no damage on the Sim and all troubleshooting has been done, then pleas edo not hesitate to contact Arigin Support.
